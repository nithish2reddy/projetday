pawan kalyan

Pawan Kalyan (born Konidela Kalyan Babu; 2 September 1968 or 1971[n 1]) is an Indian politician, actor, filmmaker, and philanthropist. He works in Telugu cinema and is the recipient of a Filmfare Award, a SIIMA Award, a CineMAA Award and a Santosham Film Award. He has featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list in the years 2013, 2017, and 2018.[5] He is also the founder of Jana Sena Party.
